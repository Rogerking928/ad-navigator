About

Why a navigator, not an encyclopedia

Most people don't want to study all of dermatology — they want to solve the problem in front of them. When a site can answer “I'm facing X, so what should I look at next?”, it's more useful than a plain encyclopedia and easier to share with others. Care content is summarized from public treatment guidelines and patient organizations (AAD, National Eczema Association, NHS, DermNet, NIH/NIAMS, ACAAI), and every path lists its sources. The “Latest research & trials” feed updates automatically from PubMed and ClinicalTrials.gov. Community voices are illustrative composites. Nothing here replaces diagnosis and treatment by your own clinician.
